Lomi Lomi Massage in Dubai

Hawaiian Healing with a European Touch

Discover the ancient art of Lomi Lomi Massage at Velvet Touch Spa – a luxurious retreat in the heart of JBR, Dubai. Rooted in traditional Hawaiian healing, this unique massage style is designed to restore balance, release emotional tension, and renew your body and spirit.

Our European massage therapists – expertly trained and certified from countries like Russia, Ukraine, Belarus – blend the flowing, rhythmic strokes of Lomi Lomi with modern wellness techniques to offer a massage unlike any other. Using forearms, elbows, and synchronized movements, they create a wave-like sensation that promotes deep relaxation and inner peace.

At Velvet Touch Spa, every Lomi Lomi session takes place in a serene and elegant spa environment, enhanced by soft lighting, soothing music, and aromatic oils. It’s not just a treatment — it’s a sacred ritual of healing and luxury.

Key Benefits:

  • Restores emotional balance and harmony
  • Improves circulation and lymphatic drainage
  • Promotes deep relaxation and stress release
  • Loosens tight muscles and supports flexibility
  • Reconnects body, mind, and soul
